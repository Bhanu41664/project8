What Is The Benefits of Knee Replacement Surgery?

    Knee replacement surgery offers several benefits, including:

  • Pain relief: It helps alleviate chronic knee pain caused by conditions like arthritis.
  • Improved mobility: The surgery enhances range of motion and makes activities like walking and climbing stairs easier.
  • Enhanced function: Knee replacement restores strength, stability, and flexibility to the knee joint, improving overall function.
  • Better quality of life: Reduced pain and increased mobility lead to an improved quality of life and the ability to engage in daily activities.
  • Long-term durability: Modern knee replacements are designed to last for many years, providing lasting benefits.
  • Psychological well-being: Relief from pain and improved function can positively impact a person's mental and emotional well-being.

MY Specialities

KNEE REPLACEMENT

Knee replacement surgery replaces parts of injured or worn-out knee joints. The surgery can help ease pain and make the knee work better. During the surgery, damaged bone and cartilage are replaced with parts made of metal and plastic.

JOINT REPLACEMENT

Total joint replacement is a surgical procedure in which parts of an arthritic or damaged joint are removed and replaced with a metal, plastic, or ceramic device called a prosthesis. The prosthesis is designed to replicate the movement of a normal, healthy joint.

FRACTURES

A fracture is a break, usually in a bone. If the broken bone punctures the skin, it is called an open or compound fracture. Fractures commonly happen because of car accidents, falls, or sports injuries. Other causes are low bone density and osteoporosis, which cause weakening of the bones.

ARTHROSCOPY & SPORTS MEDICINE

ARTHOSCOPY & SPORTS MEDICINE. Arthroscopy is a surgical procedure that is performed on joints. It is done to diagnose and treat joint problems. It is a minimally invasive procedure.
