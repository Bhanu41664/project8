Our Specializations

Dr Rohit Pandey is pioneer in CR knee replacement in Purvanchal region. He is doing primary, complex and revision Knee and Hip replacement surgeries. India is a young nation and Athletes in Purvanchal region are getting latest treatment of their sports related injuries such as ACL, PCL, Meniscus and multi ligament injuries of knee & shoulder injuries. Latest scientific world proven techniques are used and best in class care is given.
  1. Knee Arthroscopy
    Arthroscopy is a surgical procedure in which an arthroscope is inserted into the knee joint. It is a device with is used to see inside of joint. It includes a camera that is connected to a screen.
  2. Anterior Hip Replacement
    Anterior hip replacement is an approach through which no muscle are cut and it helps in faster recovery and patient have less pain. Also it is more anatomical way of doing Hip Replacement.
  3. Knee Joint Replacement
    Knee joint replacement helps patients in the treatment for severely diseased knee joints. A joint replacement procedure is designed to relieve pain, reduce symptoms, restore mobility, and regain function.
  4. ACL Reconstruction
    The procedure for anterior cruciate ligament reconstruction replaces a patient’s torn anterior cruciate ligament (ACL) tendon with a new tendon to restore knee stability.
  5. Sports Injuries
    Dr Rohit Pandey specializes in sports medicine and provides that world class care to atheletes. He specializes in sports related injuries around shoulder, ankle and other joints.
  6. Hip Resurfacing
    Hip Resurfacing also referred to as Hip Cap Replacement or Bone Conserving Surgery is a procedure that only: Replaces the hip socket and Resurfaces (reshapes) the damaged areas at the top of the thigh bone (femoral head).
